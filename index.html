Welcome to My World

Hello! My name is Jen Doyle and I welcome you to my world. I am a fun loving dynamic individual and I try to make the world around me the same. I laugh a lot. I like to meet new people and learn from them and listen to their stories. I have a lot of passions that include football, travel, reading, cooking, and music. I love to explore new places. I am always snapping pictures of something that interests me (the pictures shown are from some of the trips I have taken in the last couple of years). During the quarantine I worked on my baking skills. When I am not at home learning how to code, I can be found wandering around my neighborhood or relaxing by the lake.

Professionally, I am a highly skilled relationship builder and innovative problem solver. I am enhancing my skills in fullstack web development, so that I can use my knowledge of JavaScript, JQuery, Node, along with HTML and CSS to help create a unique experience or creatively solve problems. I am an effective communicator who actively listens to clients tell their story and am able to drill down an issue to find a creative solution. I am skilled in networking and bringing people together, as I am able to bridge gaps and build relationships between individuals and groups. I am passionate about learning people’s stories and to listen to their experiences to help create and implement their vision.
